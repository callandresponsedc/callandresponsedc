Opening

Call + Response IV will open on Saturday, October 11, 2014 at 7 p.m. at Hole in the Sky in Northeast DC. Restorations will be performing. Admission is a $5 donation at the door.

FB event: https://www.facebook.com/events/1499003413671585

Other goodies at the opening:

  • 3 Star Brewing Company will be sponsoring the opening.
  • A limited (300 copies) edition 7inch will be available, packaged with all the writing from the show for $10.
  • Milk Cult will be serving up vegetarian ramen, available for purchase.
